The Story

When bombs exploded at the Boston Marathon on April 15th, 2013, the Richard family was standing close to one of the blasts. Martin, a third grader, was killed. His sister and mother remain seriously injured. They, along with his father and brother, face a long and painful road to recovery.

Friends of the Richards, along with members of their local community, have created a campaign to support them.

About

Who?

The Richard Family Fund is coordinated through friends of the Richards.

Donations are collected via WePay and deposited directly into a special bank account administered by St. Marks Area Main Street, a nonprofit devoted to improving the St. Marks Area of Dorchester of which Bill Richard was an active member.

This site was created by members of the MIT Center for Civic Media, some of whom are friends of the Richards.

Purpose?

All money collected via this campaign will be disbursed directly to the Richards. We expect it to be used for medical expenses, for memorial services, or other appropriate expenditures at their discretion.
