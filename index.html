Martin's Story

When bombs exploded at the Boston Marathon on April 15th, 2013, the Richard family was standing close to one of the blasts. Eight-year-old Martin lost his life. His mother and sister are among the injured.

At this time of tragedy, their neighbors and the community of Dorchester have started a campaign to support the Richard family’s recovery. (Who are we?)
