Their Story

On April 15th the Richard family was watching the Boston Marathon when bombs exploded nearby. Their young son Martin was killed. His younger sister and his mother remain seriously injured.

Join us in helping the Richards along their road to recovery.

About

The Richards have not asked for help, but many people have asked how they can help the Richards. The Richard Family Fund is coordinated by friends of the family, in cooperation with their representatives, to provide an avenue of assistance.

Donations are collected via WePay and deposited directly into a special bank account administered by St. Mark's Area Main Street (not affiliated with St Mark's Church), a local nonprofit devoted to improving and assisting the community. 100% of the donations collected via this campaign will be disbursed to the Richards. We expect the funds to be used for medical expenses, for memorial services, or other appropriate expenditures at the family's discretion.

This site was crafted with love by volunteers from the MIT Center for Civic Media, some of whom are friends of the Richards.
