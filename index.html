Their Story

On April 15th the Richards were watching the Boston Marathon when bombs exploded nearby. Martin, a third grader who loved to run, was killed. His younger sister and his mother were seriously injured.

Like so many others, the Richards have suffered an unspeakable loss. Their friends, along with members of their local community, have created this campaign to support them in the healing process.

Join us in helping the Richards along their road to recovery.

About

The Richard Family Fund is coordinated by friends of the Richards and members of their local community.

Donations are collected via WePay and deposited directly into a special bank account administered by St. Marks Area Main Street, a nonprofit devoted to improving and assisting the St. Marks Area of Dorchester.

This site was created by students at the MIT Center for Civic Media, some of whom are friends of the Richards.

All donations collected via this campaign will be disbursed to the Richards. We expect the funds to be used for medical expenses, for memorial services, or other appropriate expenditures at the family's discretion.
