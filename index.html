Their Story

When bombs exploded at the Boston Marathon on April 15th, 2013, the Richard family was standing close to one of the blasts. Martin, a third grader, was killed. His sister and mother remain seriously injured. They, along with his father and brother, face a long and painful road to recovery.

Friends of the Richards, along with members of their local community, have created a campaign to support them.

About

The Richard Family Fund is coordinated by friends of the Richards.

Donations are collected via WePay and deposited directly into a special bank account administered by St. Marks Area Main Street, a nonprofit devoted to improving and assisting the part of Dorchester the Richards call home.

This site was created by students at the MIT Center for Civic Media, some of whom are friends of the Richards.

All donations collected via this campaign will be disbursed to the Richards. We expect the funds to be used for medical expenses, for memorial services, or other appropriate expenditures at the family's discretion.
